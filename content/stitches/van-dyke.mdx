# Source notes for Van Dyke

Van Dyke combines wave-like diagonal travel with pair-binds at peaks and valleys. Teaching
sequences vary; the defining feature is the two-pleat catch at each turn. Distinguish carefully
from plain wave, which does not bind pairs at the crests and troughs.
