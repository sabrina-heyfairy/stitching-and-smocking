# Source notes for Outline Stitch

Outline (English smocking) is a single-row control cord with the working thread kept on a
consistent side of the needle to produce a smooth, non-braided line. Manuals differ on whether
“outline” vs “stem” names map to thread-below vs thread-above; this guide treats outline as the
smooth cord and stem as the twisted rope, and states the needle/thread rule explicitly in diagrams.
