# Source notes for Surface Honeycomb

Surface honeycomb follows the staggered pair-bind logic of classic honeycomb but with shallower
pull and closer row spacing so the lattice sits flatter on the mountains. Elasticity is reduced
compared with classic honeycomb; treat it as decorative structure unless sampled for stretch.
