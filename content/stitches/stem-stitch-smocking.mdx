# Source notes for Stem Stitch

Smocking stem keeps the needle on the same side of the working thread (always above or always
below), producing a twisted rope rather than cable’s alternating braid. Naming vs outline varies
by manual; diagrams in this chapter state the mechanical rule rather than relying on contested names alone.
